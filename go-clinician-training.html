Revolutionizing Home Exercise Rehabilitation

We believe that improving patient outcomes can be achieved by changing behavior on a daily basis.

MedBridge GO provides daily motivation to engage your patients with their prescribed home exercise programs inside an interactive, video-based playlist.

Time-based reminders and gamified rewards keep patients on track throughout their recovery plan and beyond. An intuitive interface and easy-to-follow videos walk patients through their exercises, providing a higher quality of care.

How it Works

1. Clinician builds patient HEP

Create your patient's home exercise program in the MedBridge HEP builder and assign the proper parameters (sets, reps, hold, weekly/daily count, etc).

2. Reps convert to daily dosage

MedBridge GO converts your assigned exercises and selected parameters and will calculate the appropriate dosage into a daily program for easier adherence.

3. Clinician shares HEP with patient

Share the HEP you've created with your patient via text, email, or print out.

4. Patient activates and begins

Your patient will be prompted to download the app and will see the following messaging about exercise duration.
